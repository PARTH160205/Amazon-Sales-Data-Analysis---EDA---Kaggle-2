# 📊 Amazon Sales Data Analysis (EDA)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project performs **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** on an Amazon sales dataset to uncover insights related to product performance, customer behavior, and revenue trends. The dataset includes details such as product categories, pricing, discounts, regions, payment methods, ratings, and sales.

## 🎯 Objectives
- Understand sales and revenue distribution  
- Analyze the impact of discounts on sales  
- Identify top-performing product categories  
- Study customer payment preferences  
- Explore regional and seasonal trends  

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- Python  
- Pandas  
- NumPy  
- Matplotlib  

## 📂 Dataset Features
- `order_id` – Unique order identifier  
- `order_date` – Date of order  
- `product_id` – Product identifier  
- `product_category` – Category of product  
- `price` – Original price  
- `discount_percent` – Discount applied (%)  
- `quantity_sold` – Units sold  
- `customer_region` – Customer location  
- `payment_method` – Mode of payment  
- `rating` – Product rating  
- `review_count` – Number of reviews  
- `discounted_price` – Price after discount  
- `total_revenue` – Total revenue generated  

## 🔥 Key Insights
- Top product categories contribute the highest share of total revenue  
- Higher discounts lead to increased quantity sold  
- Digital payment methods are more preferred than cash on delivery  
- Revenue varies significantly across different regions  
- Higher-rated products generate more revenue  
- Sales show seasonal trends across months  

## 🚀 Conclusion
The EDA reveals important patterns in customer behavior and product performance, helping businesses make **data-driven decisions** regarding pricing, marketing strategies, and inventory management.
